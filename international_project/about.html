Gender Inequality in Turkey

Our Mission

Our teams mission is to end domestic violence in Turkey by empowering individuals, families, and communities to create a culture of safety, respect, and equality. We strive to raise awareness, provide support to survivors, and advocate for stronger laws and enforcement to protect those at risk. By collaborating with local organizations, policymakers, and the public, we aim to challenge the societal norms and systemic factors that perpetuate violence. Together, we work toward a future where every person can live free from fear, and homes across Turkey are spaces of peace and dignity.
