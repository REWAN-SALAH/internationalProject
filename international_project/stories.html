Summary

The three stories explore the challenges women face in Turkey, including inadequate legal protection, rising domestic violence, and societal unrest. The first story details the failure of the legal system, where an attacker returns to harass his victim despite a restraining order. The second focuses on the societal pressures driving increased violence against women, fueled by limited rights and economic opportunities. The third follows protests demanding justice for murdered women and highlights the government's inaction despite rising homicide rates.
